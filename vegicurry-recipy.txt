Ingredients:
 lots of vegetables (carrots,courgette,aubergine,paksoi,cabbage,broccoli,...)
 2 (red) onions
 150 ml cocosmilk
 1 spoon kerry powder

Directions:
 slice all the vegetables in smallish parts (like 1cm)
 bake the sliced onions for 3 minutes and add the kerry; stir
 add the cocosmilk, let it boil for 2 minutes then
 add the vegetables and close the pan with its lid.
 Stir occasionally till vegetables are en dente.
